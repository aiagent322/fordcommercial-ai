Frequently asked
Nationwide Delivery FAQ
Can I buy a Ford commercial truck from Peoria Ford if I live in another state?+
Yes. Peoria Ford regularly sells and ships commercial trucks to buyers in all 50 states. The process is fully remote — you never need to travel to Arizona. Select your truck online or by phone, handle paperwork electronically, and take delivery at the Ford dealer nearest to you. Fleet buyers can arrange direct delivery to their operations yard for orders of 5+ trucks.
How much does it cost to ship a truck to my state?+
Transport costs depend on distance and are quoted upfront before you commit. Delivery to neighboring Southwest states runs approximately $400–$800. Cross-country delivery (Arizona to Florida, for example) typically runs $900–$1,400. For most buyers, Peoria Ford's fleet pricing advantage — $2,000–$5,000 below typical retail pricing — more than covers the transport cost, making the delivered price still competitive with local options.
Can I trade in my current truck to Peoria Ford?+
Yes. Peoria Ford accepts trade-ins as part of remote commercial truck purchases. You'll submit photos and vehicle information for an appraisal, which is applied to your new truck purchase. Trade-in vehicles are typically transported to Peoria Ford after delivery of your new truck is confirmed — logistics are coordinated by the commercial sales team.
