Medium Duty Leadership

The Ford F-650 and F-750 represent Ford's medium duty commercial truck offerings, delivering Class 6 and Class 7 capability for Arizona's most demanding commercial applications. With Gross Vehicle Weight Ratings reaching 37,000 pounds and maximum payload capacities approaching 29,000 pounds, these trucks handle box truck bodies, heavy dump configurations, and specialized commercial equipment.

At Peoria Ford, the F-650 and F-750 serve Arizona commercial operators who have outgrown Super Duty capacity. Beverage distributors, furniture delivery fleets, construction companies running heavy dump operations, and municipal organizations all rely on these medium duty platforms.

F-650: Class 6 Commercial Capability

The Ford F-650 provides Class 6 ratings with GVWR options reaching 26,000 pounds, positioning it perfectly for box truck applications, medium-duty dump bodies, and commercial service platforms that exceed Super Duty capacity. The F-650's maneuverability advantages over larger Class 7 trucks make it ideal for urban delivery operations.

Popular F-650 configurations in Arizona include 16-26 foot box trucks for freight and delivery operations, landscape dump bodies with 6-8 yard capacity, and heavy-duty service platforms supporting crane and lift equipment.

F-750: Class 7 Maximum Capacity

The Ford F-750 steps up to Class 7 ratings with GVWR reaching 37,000 pounds, delivering maximum medium duty capability for operations demanding the highest payload and body capacity. For Arizona construction companies running heavy dump operations, refuse collection services, and commercial haulers, the F-750 provides capacity approaching over-the-road truck levels.

The F-750's reinforced frame, heavy-duty axles, and commercial-grade braking systems handle sustained operation at maximum ratings. Air brake configurations ensure reliable stopping power for the heaviest loads.

Powertrain Options

Ford offers both diesel and gasoline powertrains in the F-650 and F-750:

  • 6.7L Power Stroke Turbodiesel: 330 hp, 825 lb-ft torque - ideal for heavy hauling and maximum torque applications
  • 7.3L V8 Gas: 350 hp, 468 lb-ft torque - excellent for lighter-duty applications and lower maintenance costs

The diesel option excels for operators running at or near maximum weights consistently, while the gas engine serves applications with variable loads.

Commercial Body Applications

The F-650 and F-750 platforms support virtually unlimited commercial body configurations:

  • Box trucks and van bodies from 16-28 feet for freight and delivery
  • Heavy dump bodies with 8-16 cubic yard capacity
  • Refuse and recycling collection bodies
  • Flatbed and stake configurations for equipment transport
  • Tanker bodies for fuel, water, and liquid transport
  • Specialized crane and service platforms
  • Tow truck and heavy recovery platforms

Fleet Integration

The F-650 and F-750 integrate with Ford Pro telematics systems, providing fleet managers with comprehensive visibility into vehicle operation. For businesses operating mixed fleets of Super Duty and medium duty trucks, this common telematics platform simplifies fleet management.

Ford's commercial financing programs support acquisition through flexible lease structures, commercial lines of credit, and fleet purchase programs.
