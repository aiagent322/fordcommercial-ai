Medium Duty Leadership

The Ford F-650 and F-750 represent Ford's medium duty commercial truck offerings, delivering Class 6 and Class 7 capability for Arizona's most demanding commercial applications. With Gross Vehicle Weight Ratings reaching 37,000 pounds and maximum payload capacities approaching 29,000 pounds, these trucks handle box truck bodies, heavy dump configurations, and specialized commercial equipment that require true medium duty platforms.

At Peoria Ford, the F-650 and F-750 serve Arizona commercial operators who have outgrown Super Duty capacity. Beverage distributors, furniture delivery fleets, construction companies running heavy dump operations, and municipal organizations all rely on these medium duty platforms to move their businesses forward. The trucks' familiar Ford architecture simplifies driver training and maintenance compared to competitive offerings from traditional commercial truck manufacturers.

F-650: Class 6 Commercial Capability

The Ford F-650 provides Class 6 ratings with GVWR options reaching 26,000 pounds, positioning it perfectly for box truck applications, medium-duty dump bodies, and commercial service platforms that exceed Super Duty capacity. The F-650's maneuverability advantages over larger Class 7 trucks make it ideal for urban delivery operations navigating Phoenix's commercial districts and residential neighborhoods.

Popular F-650 configurations in Arizona include 16-26 foot box trucks for freight and delivery operations, landscape dump bodies with 6-8 yard capacity, and heavy-duty service platforms supporting crane and lift equipment. The truck's 22.5-inch wheels and commercial-grade suspension handle these demanding applications while providing the ride quality that keeps drivers productive throughout their shifts.

F-750: Class 7 Maximum Capacity

The Ford F-750 steps up to Class 7 ratings with GVWR reaching 37,000 pounds, delivering maximum medium duty capability for operations demanding the highest payload and body capacity. For Arizona construction companies running heavy dump operations, refuse collection services, and commercial haulers moving dense materials, the F-750 provides capacity that approaches over-the-road truck levels while maintaining the operational flexibility of a conventional commercial truck.

The F-750's reinforced frame, heavy-duty axles, and commercial-grade braking systems handle sustained operation at maximum ratings. Air brake configurations ensure reliable stopping power for the heaviest loads, while the truck's commercial electrical architecture supports auxiliary equipment including PTOs, body controls, and specialized lighting systems.

Powertrain Options

Ford offers both diesel and gasoline powertrains in the F-650 and F-750, allowing operators to select the optimal engine for their specific commercial requirements:

  • 6.7L Power Stroke Turbodiesel: 330 hp, 825 lb-ft torque - ideal for heavy hauling, towing, and applications demanding maximum torque
  • 7.3L V8 Gas: 350 hp, 468 lb-ft torque - excellent for lighter-duty applications, lower fuel costs, and simplified maintenance

The diesel option excels for operators running at or near maximum weights consistently, while the gas engine serves applications with variable loads and operators prioritizing lower acquisition and maintenance costs. Both engines pair with Ford's proven TorqShift automatic transmission for smooth, reliable operation.

Commercial Body Applications

The F-650 and F-750 platforms support virtually unlimited commercial body configurations. Common applications we deliver to Arizona operators include:

  • Heavy dump bodies with 8-16 cubic yard capacity for construction and excavation
