The Commercial Chassis Standard

The Ford F-550 Chassis Cab represents Ford's commitment to serious commercial operators who require maximum payload capacity in a maneuverable, familiar platform. With a Gross Vehicle Weight Rating of 19,500 pounds and payload capacity reaching 12,600 pounds, the F-550 handles commercial bodies and equipment that would overwhelm any pickup-based platform. For Arizona contractors, utility companies, and municipal fleets, the F-550 delivers commercial-grade capability without requiring the specialized infrastructure of true medium-duty trucks.

At Peoria Ford's commercial department, the F-550 Chassis Cab consistently serves as the foundation for Arizona's most demanding commercial upfits. From heavy-duty dump bodies hauling construction materials across Phoenix job sites to fully-equipped service trucks supporting utility operations throughout Maricopa County, the F-550 provides the robust platform these applications demand.

Cab-to-Axle Flexibility

The F-550 Chassis Cab offers multiple cab-to-axle configurations ranging from 60 to 84 inches, providing upfitters with the flexibility to mount virtually any commercial body. This dimensional flexibility means the same platform can support compact service bodies for urban operations or extended flatbeds for oversized cargo transport. Peoria Ford works with Arizona's premier upfitters to specify the optimal cab-to-axle dimension for each customer's specific commercial body requirements.

The chassis frame rails extend cleanly behind the cab, providing solid mounting points for commercial bodies and equipment. Ford's body builder guide ensures upfitters understand exactly where mounting holes, electrical connections, and auxiliary provisions are located, streamlining the upfit process and reducing installation time.

Heavy-Duty Upfit Configurations

The F-550 platform supports commercial applications that lighter trucks simply cannot handle. Common configurations we deliver to Arizona commercial operators include:

  • Contractor dump bodies with 4-6 cubic yard capacity for excavation and landscaping
  • Heavy-duty service bodies with integrated crane systems rated to 6,000+ pounds
  • Flatbed configurations with gooseneck hitches for equipment transport
  • Aerial lift platforms for utility and tree service operations
  • Enclosed service bodies with extensive compartmentalization for specialized trades
  • Water trucks and spray systems for dust control and landscaping
  • Fuel and lube service trucks for mobile equipment maintenance

Power Stroke Commercial Performance

The F-550 exclusively pairs with Ford's 6.7-liter Power Stroke turbodiesel, delivering 475 horsepower and 1,050 lb-ft of torque optimized for sustained commercial operation. Unlike configurations that offer gas engine alternatives, Ford engineers specifically matched the diesel powerplant to the F-550's commercial mission, ensuring reliable performance regardless of load or operating conditions.

The Power Stroke's exhaust brake provides engine retardation that dramatically extends service brake life—a critical consideration for dump trucks and other applications involving frequent stops under heavy loads. Arizona's mountainous terrain makes this feature particularly valuable for commercial operators working routes with significant grade changes.

Commercial Electrical Architecture

The F-550 Chassis Cab features commercial-grade electrical provisions that support the most demanding upfit requirements. Dual alternators provide the charging capacity necessary for aerial lifts, welding equipment, and other high-draw accessories. Pre-wired connections for PTO operation, body lighting, and auxiliary equipment simplify upfit installation and ensure reliable long-term performance.

Ford's commercial electrical architecture includes provisions for telematics integration, allowing fleet managers to monitor vehicle location, driver behavior, and maintenance requirements through Ford Pro systems. For businesses operating multiple F-550s across dispersed Arizona job sites, these tools provide the visibility necessary to optimize operations and control costs.
