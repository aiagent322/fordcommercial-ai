Purpose-Built for Commercial Upfits

The Ford F-550 Chassis Cab represents the pinnacle of Ford's Super Duty commercial offerings, delivering a purpose-built platform designed specifically for professional upfit integration. Unlike pickup-based configurations that require adaptation for commercial bodies, the F-550 arrives from the factory as a bare chassis ready for immediate mounting of dump bodies, service bodies, flatbeds, and specialized commercial equipment. For Arizona businesses requiring maximum payload capacity and upfit flexibility, the F-550 stands alone.

At Peoria Ford's commercial department, we've built our reputation around F-550 expertise. From excavation contractors requiring reliable dump trucks to utility companies needing bucket truck platforms, the F-550 serves as Arizona's go-to commercial chassis. The truck's Class 5 rating with a maximum GVWR of 19,500 pounds accommodates heavy commercial bodies while remaining below CDL thresholds in most configurations—a critical consideration for businesses managing driver licensing requirements.

Chassis Configurations for Every Application

Ford engineers the F-550 in multiple wheelbase configurations to match specific body requirements. Regular cab models with shorter wheelbases serve well for dump body applications where maneuverability matters. Extended cab and crew cab configurations provide additional seating for crews while maintaining the frame length necessary for longer service bodies. Available wheelbases range from 141 inches to over 200 inches, ensuring proper weight distribution regardless of body type.

The F-550's frame construction utilizes fully-boxed high-strength steel rails designed to support commercial body mounting without additional reinforcement. Factory-installed body builder wiring simplifies electrical integration, while standardized mounting points align with industry specifications used by major upfitters. These engineering decisions dramatically reduce upfit time and cost compared to adapting pickup-based platforms.

Power Stroke Commercial Performance

The F-550 exclusively pairs with Ford's 6.7-liter Power Stroke turbodiesel engine, delivering the torque and durability commercial applications demand. The engine's 1,050 lb-ft of torque moves fully-loaded dump bodies without strain, while the sophisticated exhaust brake reduces service brake wear during loaded descents. For Arizona operators running dump trucks in mountainous terrain or hauling heavy service bodies across the Phoenix metro area, the Power Stroke's commercial-grade performance proves essential.

Ford's 10-speed TorqShift automatic transmission includes commercial calibrations that optimize shift patterns for loaded operation. The transmission holds gears longer under load, reducing unnecessary shifting that creates wear and driver fatigue. Power take-off provisions support hydraulic pumps for dump bodies, aerial equipment, and other PTO-driven accessories without aftermarket modifications.

Commercial Electrical Architecture

Operating commercial bodies and auxiliary equipment demands robust electrical systems, and the F-550 delivers with commercial-grade architecture. Heavy-duty alternators produce the sustained amperage necessary for lighting systems, power tools, and electronic equipment. Multiple battery configurations support high-draw starting in extreme temperatures while maintaining reserves for accessory operation.

The factory body builder wiring package provides pre-installed circuits for auxiliary lighting, backup alarms, and PTO controls. These provisions significantly reduce upfit labor costs and ensure electrical integration meets Ford's engineering standards. For fleet operators, consistent electrical architecture across multiple F-550 units simplifies maintenance training and parts inventory.
