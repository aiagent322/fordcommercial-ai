The Foundation of Commercial Capability

The Ford F-250 Super Duty represents the entry point into Ford's legendary Super Duty lineup, delivering the perfect balance of capability, maneuverability, and value for commercial operators across Arizona and the Southwest.

At Peoria Ford, we've seen thousands of Arizona businesses build their operations around the F-250. From HVAC contractors who need to tow heavy equipment to plumbers requiring service body upfits, the F-250 is the most popular commercial truck in our inventory.

Powertrain Options Built for Work

The standard 7.3-liter V8 gas engine delivers 430 horsepower and 485 lb-ft of torque. For maximum pulling power, the 6.7-liter Power Stroke turbodiesel provides 475 horsepower and 1,050 lb-ft of torque.

Commercial Upfit Ready

Every F-250 arrives ready for immediate upfit integration:

  • Reading and Knapheide service bodies
  • Stake bed configurations for landscaping
  • Enclosed utility bodies for contractors
  • Custom tool storage and ladder racks
  • Towing packages with brake controllers

Fleet Management Integration

Ford Pro Telematics integration allows fleet managers to monitor vehicle location, driver behavior, fuel consumption, and maintenance schedules through a single dashboard.

Arizona-Proven Durability

The extreme Arizona summer heat tests every component. Ford engineers the Super Duty line specifically for severe-duty applications with upgraded cooling systems and heavy-duty electrical components.
