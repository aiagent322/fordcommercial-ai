What Ford Pro includes
Four Core Telematics Capabilities

Live GPS Tracking

Know where every vehicle is in real time — updated continuously. Dispatch the nearest truck, verify arrival times, and eliminate "where's the truck" calls. Geofencing alerts notify you when vehicles enter or leave defined areas.

Vehicle Health Alerts

Ford Pro monitors engine diagnostics, DTC codes, fluid levels, tire pressure, and battery status across your fleet. Proactive alerts notify fleet managers before a minor issue becomes a breakdown — reducing unplanned downtime and emergency repair costs.

Driver Behavior Insights

Monitor speeding, harsh braking, rapid acceleration, and idle time across every driver. Coaching reports help fleet managers identify high-risk drivers, reduce accident exposure, and lower insurance premiums through documented behavior improvement.

Fuel Monitoring

Track fuel consumption per vehicle, driver, and route. Identify waste from excessive idling, inefficient routing, and unauthorized use. Ford Pro telematics typically reduces fleet fuel costs 8–15% in the first year through data-driven operational changes.
