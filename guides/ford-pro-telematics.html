What Ford Pro Intelligence Does

Real-Time GPS Tracking

See every vehicle's exact location on a live map — updated continuously. Dispatchers can find the nearest truck, verify routes, and review 90-day location history. Geofencing alerts notify you when trucks leave or enter defined zones.

Driver Behavior Scoring

Every driver is scored on hard braking, rapid acceleration, excessive idling, and speeding. Fleet managers receive weekly scorecards. Most fleets reduce fuel costs 8–12% within 90 days through driver coaching alone.

Fuel Consumption Analytics

Track fuel economy per vehicle, per driver, and per route. Identify underperforming trucks — often the first sign of a maintenance issue — before they become expensive breakdowns.

Predictive Maintenance Alerts

Ford Pro reads engine load, coolant temp, battery voltage, and transmission behavior continuously — alerting you before fault codes become breakdowns. Scheduled maintenance reminders prevent deferred maintenance that shortens vehicle life.

DTC Fault Code Monitoring

When a check engine light appears on any vehicle, Ford Pro identifies the DTC code and severity instantly. Critical faults trigger immediate alerts; minor codes are logged for next scheduled service.

Remote Diagnostics

Ford service technicians can access vehicle diagnostic data remotely — enabling faster, more accurate repair estimates without the vehicle coming in first. Reduces shop time and fleet downtime.

Ford Pro Intelligence FAQ
What is Ford Pro Intelligence?+
Ford Pro Intelligence is Ford's built-in fleet telematics platform for commercial vehicles. It connects through a factory-installed modem on most 2022+ Ford commercial vehicles and transmits GPS location, vehicle health, driver behavior, and fuel data to a cloud dashboard — no additional hardware required on compatible vehicles.
How does Ford Pro compare to Samsara or Geotab?+
Ford Pro Intelligence integrates natively at the factory level, providing deeper vehicle data than aftermarket OBD-II plug-ins. Samsara and Geotab offer multi-brand fleet support and more advanced third-party integrations. For all-Ford commercial fleets, Ford Pro provides the most complete data picture at the lowest total cost.
