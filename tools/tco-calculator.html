How to use TCO in decisions
Why TCO Beats Purchase Price

Diesel Premium Payback

The 6.7L Power Stroke diesel costs ~$11,000 more upfront but delivers better fuel economy and higher resale value. At 25,000 miles/year, most high-mileage operators recover that premium within 3–4 years — making diesel the better 5-year TCO choice.

Right-Sizing Saves Money

An over-spec'd F-550 doing F-350 work costs $25,000+ more up front with higher operating costs annually. The right-sized truck for the application almost always delivers the lowest 5-year TCO.

Section 179 Front-Loads Value

A $76,000 F-450 at 28% combined tax rate saves ~$21,000 in year one via Section 179 — effectively reducing your net outlay by 28% and dramatically improving first-year cash flow vs conventional depreciation.
