Why the math matters
The 3 Numbers Every Buyer Must Know

GVWR

Gross Vehicle Weight Rating — the maximum safe operating weight of the fully loaded truck. Set by Ford engineers. Your payload capacity is GVWR minus the truck's curb weight. Exceeding GVWR is a DOT violation and voids your warranty.

Payload Rating

The weight you can legally carry — crew, cargo, tools, and body combined. Printed on the door-jamb sticker. The calculator uses published maximum payload values; your specific truck may vary based on options and configuration.

Body Weight

The most overlooked factor. A Knapheide steel service body weighs 1,400–1,800 lbs before you put a single tool in it. Always subtract body weight from rated payload to find your actual usable cargo capacity.
