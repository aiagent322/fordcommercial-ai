The Real Power of Section 179

What qualifies?

All Ford Super Duty (F-250 through F-750) and Transit models qualify. GVWR must exceed 6,000 lbs and business use must exceed 50%.

100% year-one deduction

Instead of depreciating over 5 years, you deduct the full purchase price in year one — dramatically reducing your tax bill when you need it most.

Real example

A $75,000 F-350 at 28% combined tax rate saves ~$21,000 in taxes — making your effective net cost just $54,000. That's a real $21K discount for buying the truck you need.
