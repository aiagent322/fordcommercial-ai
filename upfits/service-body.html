Common questions
Service Body Truck FAQ
What is a service body truck?+
A service body truck replaces the standard pickup bed with a purpose-built steel or aluminum utility body featuring lockable side compartments, top storage, and sometimes a crane or liftgate. They're used by service professionals who need organized, secure, mobile storage for tools and equipment on job sites.
Which Ford model is best for a service body?+
The F-350 chassis cab is the professional standard for most service body applications. It provides sufficient payload for a full-size Knapheide body plus a full complement of tools and materials. For heavier applications — large crane trucks, heavy rescue bodies — the F-450 or F-550 chassis is the right platform.
